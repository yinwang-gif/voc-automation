# Seller（谁真的需要）

Define who cares first, why they care now, how to explain the value, and how to test willingness to act.

## Do

- Define initial ICP or target adopter.
- Identify current alternative and switching cost.
- Write positioning and pitch only when appropriate.
- Identify objections.
- Design low-cost seller experiments or internal adoption tests.
- Route external products, internal tools, and customer co-creation differently.
- Add lane evidence fields.

## Do Not

- Assume "users want efficiency" is enough.
- Use internal product language as customer language.
- Count compliments as validation.
- Treat internal belief as enough for `pilot` or `enter_prd`.
- Skip objections.
